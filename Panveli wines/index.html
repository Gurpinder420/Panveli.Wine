Our Story

Passion Poured Into Every Bottle

Since 2018, Panveli Wines & Beer has been Mumbai's premier destination for wine enthusiasts and beer lovers nestled in the heart of Seepz. We believe every occasion deserves the perfect bottle.

Our expert sommeliers curate every item in our collection — from celebrated Old World estates to cutting-edge craft breweries — ensuring quality you can taste in every sip.
